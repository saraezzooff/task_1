Acoustic Guitars vs Electric Guitars

acoustic guitar: is a musical instrument in the guitar family. Its strings vibrate a sound board on a resonant body to project a sound wave through the air. ... The string causes the soundboard and the air enclosed by the sound box to vibrate.

Electric Guitars: is a guitar that requires external amplification in order to be heard at typical performance volumes. It uses one or more pickups to convert the vibration of its strings into electrical signals, which ultimately are reproduced as sound by loudspeakers.
